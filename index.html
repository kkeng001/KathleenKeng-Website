My name is Kathleen Keng and I am a first year studying at the University of California, Riverside. I am preparing my undergraduate prerequisites before joining UCR's School of Business. I want to center my studies in the field of management and take my learnings and bachelor degree to become an Executive Manager or an Entrepreneur. The reason why I chose to study business is because this whole field can be resourceful to many parts. Like if one plan doesn't work, you could always move on to another. I always like to be organized and work around others but I also like to do things on my own.

At UC Riverside, I have been involved with a cultural organization called Katipunan. It is a Pilipino based organization but they accept anyone of race whether they are Filipino or not. I am Cambodian myself so I was afraid I wouldn't be able to fit in. But I never felt that as they were all so inclusive. Recently, I was elected and voted to be the next school year's Treasurer for Katipunan as I will be the one to handle all the money transactions throughout the year. I hope to gain more experience and learnings from this position and to grow as a leader and work well as a teammate.

Outside of UCR, I work part-time at Sharetea back at my home in Temecula. It is franchised by my mom so I would be in charge of scheduling and contacting the employees. This has allowed me to be a team leader and to work in a professional environment.
